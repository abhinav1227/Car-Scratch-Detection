About

This project focuses on Car Scratch Detection, in sync with the development of autonomous quality inspection systems of different types of products. For example in a parking lot such detection provides the client with the assurance that their personal property will not be affected inside the lot.

  • We made use of Transfer Learning, wherein we started with Mask RCNN but due to run complexity we shifted towards Yolov5. This being a single class detection problem.
  • The dataset consists of 886 images, collected from various sources. Annotation of such data was a bit tricky since Yolo doesn't work well with a small bounding box.
  • We started with a mAP50(average of AP i.e area under the precision recall at IOU threshold 0.5) of around 0.2 and reached 0.542. Precision and Recall were 0.62 and 0.59 which shows a good trade-off.
